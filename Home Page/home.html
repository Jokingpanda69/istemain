About

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is the leading National Professional non-profit making Society for the Technical Education System in our country with the motto of Career Development of Teachers and Personality Development of Students and overall development of our Technical Education System.

Mission

To foster excellence in technical education, nurture innovation, and facilitate holistic development of educators and students, thereby fostering a dynamic learning environment that cultivates critical thinking, creativity, and adaptability

Vision

To be a transformative force in technical education, empowering educators, nurturing students into skilled professionals with exemplary character, leadership qualities, and driving ongoing enhancement of India's technical education sector.
